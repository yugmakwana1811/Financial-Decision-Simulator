LEARN THE CONCEPTS

Understand the words behind the numbers

EMI, principal, rate and tenure

An EMI is a monthly loan instalment. Principal is the amount borrowed; interest is the cost of borrowing; tenure is the number of monthly instalments.

Liquidity, emergency fund and resilience

Liquidity means money available immediately. Emergency coverage shows how many months of essential expenses available savings could cover. Stress testing asks how these values change under hypothetical setbacks.

Assumptions and projections

A future-value or savings-path result is a projection, not a guarantee. It depends on stated assumptions such as constant income, expenses, prices, and growth rate.
